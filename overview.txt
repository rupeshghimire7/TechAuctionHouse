TechAuctionHouse

An e-commerce web application where tech related products are listed by people and allows other people to bid to the item/s they want and the highest bid gets the product/s.

This web-app has features like:

Login Page
Home Page : List of all categories and few people's favourite items under each categories
Category related page
Item page
Cart page on winning auction
Payment page

For each item:
	--displayed as a card
	--show highest bid
	--time until the bidding ends
	--contains cover image of item, product id, best-bid-price, time-remaining, available/sold status
